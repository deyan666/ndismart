FAQ

Common questions



How does billing work?

You're billed monthly via Stripe. Cancel any time from the billing portal and your featured status continues until the end of your billing period.

When does my listing go live?

Featured status is applied within minutes of payment. You'll appear at the top of relevant search results almost immediately.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes — no lock-in contracts. Use the "Manage Subscription" tab above to access your Stripe portal and cancel with one click.

What payment methods are accepted?

Visa, Mastercard, Amex, and Apple Pay / Google Pay via Stripe's secure checkout.

I'm already listed — do I need to sign up again?

No. If you're in the NDIS provider register your basic listing already exists. Purchasing Featured upgrades your existing listing.

What's the difference between Featured and Premium?

Featured gets you top placement and the gold badge. Premium adds a dedicated full-profile page, logo upload, and monthly performance reports.
